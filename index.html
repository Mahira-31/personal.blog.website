EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT MET GALA 2024


It's fashion's biggest night! The Met Gala 2024 red carpet is officially underway
at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Tonight, guests will ascend the museum's famed steps wearing outfits that
nod to the event's “The Garden of Time” dress code, and you can see all of
the Met Gala 2024 looks right here as the night unfolds.

The gala, which fundraises for the Metropolitan Museum's Costume Institute,
is known for having a very secretive guest list.

But while the attendees' names are kept tightly under wraps, we do know of a
few people who will grace the carpet in their garden party finery: Bad Bunny,
Chris Hemsworth, Jennifer Lopez, and Zendaya, who are serving as co-chairs
alongside Vogue's Anna Wintour.

The rest of the esteemed attendees—which includes the world's top singers,
actors, models, athletes, and artists—will reveal themselves in due time.

Below, see every look from the 2024 Met Gala red carpet, and check back in
throughout the night for updates, or follow along in real time on our
livestream or live blog.
